Musician App

MusicianApp is a web based collaborative musical instrument that makes it easy for anyone to get started making original music, by composing, collaborating and even recording.

It was created by a team of 3 people as part of the CUNY Tech Prep program. On this project, I created the layout, and worked on the design. We used tone.js to help with sound generation, and nexus ui to help with the instrument design. I also implemented the authentication, so that users can create an account and login.

Showtrakr

Showtrakr is a TV Tracker web app. It gives users the ability to keep track of their progress while they watch a season of their favorite TV program. Users can create an account, log in, search for their favorite show, and then track their progress as they watch it.

This project was created by a team of 4 people for the CUNY Codes program. Personally, I worked on the front end, using React.js, and on the layout and design, using bootstrap and custom CSS.
