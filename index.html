Code

CapCoin

CapCoin is a cryptocurrency, built from scratch. It was the capstone project for my Computer Science degree at Hunter College. It includes a full-node in the form of a desktop app, written in C++ and C, as well as a lightweight web-app wallet, written in JavaScript, HTML, CSS and C.

I worked alongside a team of 4 other computer science students, and contributed primarily to the front-end of the lightweight wallet, and the network and wallet of the full-node. I also functioned as the group manager, conducted weekly standup meetings, and created a timeline of milestones to ensure that the MVP was completed on time.

Code: https://github.com/David-Bohl/capcoin

Musician App

MusicianApp is a web based collaborative musical instrument that makes it easy for anyone to get started making original music, by composing, collaborating and even recording.

It was created by a team of 3 people as part of the CUNY Tech Prep program. On this project, I came up with the original concept, created the layout and user friendly responsive design, implemented user signup and login, and helped to build the synth, drum machine and effects. I also played a large role in managing the team. We used tone.js to help with sound generation, and nexus ui to help with the instrument design.

Live Site: MusicianApp
Code: https://github.com/rupeshbasnet/MusicianApp

Showtrakr

Showtrakr is a TV Tracker web app. It gives users the ability to keep track of their progress while they watch a season of their favorite TV program. Users can create an account, log in, search for their favorite show, and then track their progress as they watch it.

This project was created by a team of 4 people for the CUNY Codes coding mentorship program. Personally, I worked on the front end, using React.js, and on the layout and design, using bootstrap and custom CSS.

Code: https://github.com/David-Bohl/showtrack
